Overview

Our Peer-Teaching program is a 3 year academic and leadership building program. Student Leaders act as tutors for learners from lower grades and aim to bridge their academic gaps. In the process SLs develop future ready skills

Our Model

The first two years of our program require strong operations support. For this purpose we initiated the Changemaker fellowship. Fellows under this program join us part-time to manage Peer teaching operations in schools. These fellows gain exposure into the education sector and get a chance to work in a professional environment while still in college.
