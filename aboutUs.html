OUR STORY

  1. 2017: Idea for Involve wins the Global Engagement Summit at Northwestern University Chicago.

    01

  2. 2018: Involve registered as an organization and year long Peer Teaching pilot implemented with 200 students.

    02

  3. 2018: Involve recognized as one of the top 10 non-profits in India by N/Core and gets incubated at Singapore International Foundation's YSE program.

    03

  4. 2019: Peer Teaching program conducted in 30 schools in Chennai and Bengaluru, impacting 2400 students.

    04

  5. 2020: Online Peer Teaching pilot with 30 student groups teaching and learning from one another.

    05

  6. 2020: Involves Peer Teaching program shortlisted for Reimagine Education Presence Teaching and Learning award.

    06
