Our Programs

Peer based learning is a pedagogical practice that improves ownership and investment in learning amoong students. When students teach and learn from their peers, on one hand they develop better clarity about the content being taught, while on the other hand they develop skills like collaboration, problem solving and become stronger communicators. .

Offline Peer teaching

Our in school Peer Teaching program prepared students from Grade 7 and above to teach their juniors (Grade 4-7). Student Leaders ( senior students) are given training in content and given the resources and tools to create an engaging classroom for their learners. Besides this leadership sessions are conducted with Student Leaders to help them reflect on their learning of skills and mindsets.

Online Peer teaching

With schools being shut during the COVID-19 pandemic, the learning of many of our students came to a complete hault. In order to prevent loss of learning, we decided to start our programs online. At the outset, we ensured that we were able to conenct with all our students and began a simple Learn from Home program, where students received daily activiteis throughs WhatsApp which they could do while at home. The high engagement rates for that program, gave us the confidence to start the Online Peer Teaching program. With the similar structure as our In school peer teaching program, this program focussed on getting students commited to learning everyday and building basic reading fluency and arithmetic competence in them.

Peer Learning adoption

Teachers play a pivotal role in our education system. They have the highest engagement with students. We believed that if we can leverage our teachers, to make our classrooms more student centric, we could look at a systemic change. With this thought in mind, we created teacher training modules on Peer learning strategies and facilitation. We are partnering with teacher training organizations to conduct these trainings with teachers in government and affordable private schools.
